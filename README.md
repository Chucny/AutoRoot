# ⚡ AutoRoot
Anyone can root a device with no technical understanding, with AutoRoot. <br> AutoRoot is a powerful tool made to simplify the rooting process of android devices and automate most of it. 
## 🔍 Automatic Boot Image Finder
The application redirects you to your browser and Pixel Firmware downloading page and tells you excactly what Build ID you have, taking the guesswork out of finding the right boot image.
AutoRoot also supports non-Pixel devices, but sadly, you have to find your boot image yourself.
## 🚀 Automatic Flasher
AutoRoot flashes your boot image and unlocks your bootloader automatically. It also knows what to flash by determiting your Android version and phone model.
## 🪄 Auto-Magisk installer and patcher
AutoRoot installs Magisk on your device automatically and pulls your patched boot image ready for flashing in an automated process.
## ⌨️ The enter-mechanic
For each step, you just got to press enter and the tool does the step automatically. Sometimes you have to follow simple instructions from the tool if you have to do something manually, for example enabling USB debugging etc.
